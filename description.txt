==============================
 Magician for Balatro
==============================

Tilt every probability in Balatro in your favor. Magician gives you a single
tunable "luck meter" that biases rarities, editions, seals, tags, vouchers,
pack sizes, Soul/Black Hole rolls, and every random trigger (Lucky Card,
8-Ball, Bloodstone, Sixth Sense, Wheel of Fortune...) toward the outcome you
want. Push the slider to 100% and maximum luck really means maximum luck:
1-in-5 becomes 5-in-5, 1-in-15 becomes 15-in-15, Glass cards stop shattering,
and Gros Michel refuses to go extinct.

------------------------------
 WHAT IT DOES
------------------------------

- Joker Rarity Luck
  Rolls Rare jokers far more often. At 100% intensity, every rolled joker is
  Rare.

- Biased Towards Your Run
  Reads your current deck (rank counts, suit counts, most-played hand, owned
  jokers, editioned jokers) and classifies it into one of 11 meta archetypes
  (Baron / face deck, Flush stack, Fibonacci chain, Blueprint copy core,
  edition stack, economy, stone deck, etc.), then steers shop jokers toward
  that archetype's best picks.

- Edition Luck
  Boosts Foil / Holo / Polychrome chances, with an option to also include
  Negative.

- Seal Luck
  Dramatically increases seal spawn rates.

- Tag Luck
  Rerolls skip tags to find the rarest available option.

- Voucher Luck
  Rerolls shop vouchers for a fresh pick.

- Pack Size Luck
  Upgrades Normal boosters to Jumbo and Jumbo to Mega. At max, every pack is
  Mega.

- Soul / Black Hole Luck
  Makes The Soul and Black Hole far more likely to appear inside Arcana and
  Spectral packs.

- Hits Luck
  Boosts every probability-based joker / enhancement trigger in the game:
    * Lucky Card        (+20 mult, +$20)
    * 8-Ball            (Tarot on a played 8)
    * Space Joker       (level up the played hand)
    * Business Card     ($2 on face cards)
    * Reserved Parking  ($1 on face cards in hand)
    * Bloodstone        (x1.5 mult on Heart)
    * Sixth Sense       (Spectral from the first played 6)
    * Seance            (Spectral on Straight Flush)
    * Hit the Road      (on Jack discard)
    * Hallucination     (Tarot on pack skip)
    * Wheel of Fortune  (edition on a joker)

- No Negative Hits
  Protects you from the bad random rolls:
    * Gros Michel never goes extinct.
    * Cavendish never goes extinct.
    * Glass cards don't shatter after scoring.

------------------------------
 TWO SLIDERS
------------------------------

Intensity (%)
  The main luck meter. Controls every feature's strength. 0% disables all
  bias, 100% maxes every feature out.

Shrink (%)
  Secondary knob for how aggressively the Hits Luck / Soul Luck / No
  Negative Hits systems collapse probability rolls. At 100% Shrink +
  100% Intensity, probabilistic triggers fire 100% of the time. Drop
  Shrink below 100% if you want Intensity 100% everywhere except the hit
  triggers.

------------------------------
 HOW TO OPEN THE SETTINGS
------------------------------

There are four places you can open Magician's panel:

  1. F6 anywhere - opens / closes the overlay on top of whatever screen you
     are on.
  2. The Mods menu -> Magician -> Config.
  3. The Settings menu -> new "Magician" tab (alongside Game / Video /
     Graphics / Audio).
  4. In-run pause menu (Esc) -> "Magician" button.

------------------------------
 MASTER ENABLED
------------------------------

A single top-right toggle kills every effect of the mod instantly without
losing your settings. Useful for A/B testing a run or playing legit without
uninstalling.

------------------------------
 DESIGN PRINCIPLES
------------------------------

- Run-seed reproducibility is preserved. The mod only touches the probability
  rolls it explicitly opts into - card draws, shop generation pools, enemy
  blinds, and so on are untouched.
- Everything is opt-in per feature. Don't want edition bias? Turn it off.
  The intensity slider still governs everything else.
- Linear scaling. Every feature at 50% Intensity gives roughly half its
  maximum effect, so you can tune exactly how "cheated" the run feels.

------------------------------
 COMPATIBILITY
------------------------------

Requires Steamodded (Lovely + SMODS). Works alongside other mods; Magician
only wraps vanilla probability hooks and the settings UI, so it should not
collide with content mods that add jokers, decks, or stakes.

------------------------------
 INSTALLATION
------------------------------

  1. Install Steamodded.
  2. Drop the Magician folder into %AppData%\Balatro\Mods\.
  3. Launch the game. Magician appears in the Mods list and a new tab in
     Settings.
